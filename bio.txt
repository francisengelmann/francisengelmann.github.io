Francis Engelmann is a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich with Prof. Marc Pollefeys and a visiting researcher with Federico Tombari at Google Zurich. His research in computer science focuses on computer vision and 3D scene understanding. Before joining ETH Zurich, he received his Ph.D. at RWTH Aachen with Prof. Bastian Leibe and spent some time at Google X in Munich, Google Research in Zurich, and Apple in California. He is a Fellow of the ETH AI Center, a member of the ELLIS Society, recipient of the ETHZ Career Seed Award and SNSF Postdoc.Mobility fellowship.
