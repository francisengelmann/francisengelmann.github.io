Francis Engelmann

I am an incoming assistant professor at the University of Lugano (USI) in Switzerland. I am a postdoc at Stanford University with Prof. Leonidas Guibas and Prof. Jeannette Bohg. Before that I was a postdoctoral research fellow at ETH Zurich working with Prof. Marc Pollefeys, and a visiting researcher with Federico Tombari at Google Zurich. Before ETH Zurich, I finished my PhD in the Computer Vision Group of Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe at RWTH Aachen University and spent some time at Google X in Munich with Martin Bokeloh and Google Research Zurich with Kostas Rematas, as well as Apple in California.
