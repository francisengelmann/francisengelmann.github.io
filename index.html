Francis Engelmann

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at ETH Zurich AI Center working with Prof. Dr. Siyu Tang and Prof. Dr. Otmar Hilliges. I am also a visiting researcher with Federico Tombari at Google Zurich. Before joining ETH Zurich, I finished my Ph.D. in Computer Vision, Machine Learning and 3D Scene Understanding in the Computer Vision Group of Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe at RWTH Aachen University and spent some time at Google X in Munich with Martin Bokeloh and Google Research Zurich with Kostas Rematas, as well as Apple in California.

       

News

  • January 2023 Our paper Mask3D was accepted at ICRA'23.
  • December 2022 I received the ETH Zurich Career Seed Award.
  • October 2022 I joined the group of Federico Tombari at Google Zurich for a research visit.
  • August 2022 Our Mask3D approach ranks 1st on ScanNet, ScanNet200 and 2nd on STPLS3D.
  • August 2022 One paper accepted at the ECCV 2022 AVVision Workshop.
  • July 2022 I will serve as senior program committee (SPC) for AAAI 2023.
  • July 2022 Our paper Box2Mask on weakly supervised 3D instance segmentation using bounding box annotations is accepted as oral (2.7%) to ECCV 2022 in Tel Aviv.
  • June 2022 Gave an invited talk on the future of 3D segmentation at the CVPR 2022 ScanNet workshop in New Orleans.
  • June 2022 Our winning entry on 2D floorplan reconstruction from large-scale point clouds was presented at CV4AEC with Ekin Celikkan and Theodora Kontogianni.
  • June 2022 Our model Mix3D won first place on the ScanNet 3D Semantic Segmentaion challenge with Alexey Nekrasov and Jonas Schult.
  • May 2022 I will co-organize the HBHA workshop on human body, hands, and activities from egocentric and multi-view cameras at ECCV 2022.

Projects

  • This is the online demo of Mask3D our state-of-the-art 3D semantic instance segmentation method. You can upload your own 3D scan or reconstruction (.ply) and segment the scene into the semantic classes of ScanNet, including ScanNet 200.
    Link

  • PyViz3D is a simple web-based visualizer for 3D point clouds and other primitives such as meshes, polylines, bounding boxes and labels. It is useful for quick protyping and remote development.
    Code Documentation


  • Virtual KITTI 3D: Extension of the Virtual Kitti dataset including 3D cloud representations and refined semantic labels.
    Code

  • Graph Convolutional Nets: Implementation of Kipf et al. graph convolutional networks.
    Code

  • Fast Voxel Traversal: Implementation of the Fast Voxel Traversal algorithm.
    Code
